Jordan 4 Retro Wahlburguers

Last Sold For: 100.000 US$

This pair of ultra-exclusive Air Jordan IVs were first spotted on the actor Mark Wahlberg’s feet at the June 2018 opening of his restaurant, Wahlburger’s at the Mall of America. Though the sneakers were initially a custom pair made for Mark Wahlberg, there was an unexpected public demand for a release of the collaboration. In Fall 2018 Nike created 30 pairs of the sneakers for Wahlberg to give away to a few lucky fans through a charity contest. The money was raised in collaboration with the Entertainment Industry Foundation to be given away to first responders and victims of the California wildfires. Winners were also given the opportunity to video chat with the actor.
