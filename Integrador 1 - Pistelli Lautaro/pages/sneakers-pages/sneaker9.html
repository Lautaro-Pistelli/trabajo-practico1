Jordan 4 Retro Eminem Encore (2017)

Last Sold For: 30.000 US$

Considered one of the rarest releases of Air Jordan IV’s, the Eminem “ENCORE” has become a long sought after addition to many sneakerheads collections. Due to the original release's popularity, this retro pair of Nike Air Jordan IV Retro Eminem “ENCORE” were re-released in 2017 to coincide with Eminem’s “Revival” album. This release would be featured exclusively on StockX, with only 23 pairs produced making it even more exclusive than the 2005 OG release. The 2017 Air Jordan IV Retro Eminem “ENCORE” features a slightly lighter shade of grey on the heel tab and wings, as well as a lighter shade of blue throughout but all other details stay true to the original release.
